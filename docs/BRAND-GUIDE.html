"You don't have to figure out what you need. Tell me what's going on and I'll handle it."
Voice rules
✓ Do
Short sentences with energy
Em dashes for punch
"I" not "we" — she's a person
Lead with the benefit
Occasional "listen" or "honestly"
Specific over vague, always
✗ Don't
"Notarization solutions"
"We strive to provide"
"Your satisfaction is our priority"
Passive voice
Corporate filler
Re-explaining what a notary is
UPL reminder: In Virginia, notaries cannot explain legal content, interpret document terms, or advise on whether terms are favorable. Copy should say "I'll direct you through every page" — not "I'll explain what you're signing." When in doubt, direct clients to their lender or attorney.

Meta desc
Apostille and document authentication in Richmond VA. State & federal. Immigration documents welcome. Full chain managed. Fast turnaround.
H1
International paperwork is confusing. I handle it.
Plain-language explainer
An apostille is a certificate that authenticates the origin of a public document so it's legally recognized in another country. Virginia is a member of the Hague Convention — Virginia-issued apostilles are recognized in 120+ countries. For non-Hague countries, a different process (federal authentication) applies. I'll tell you exactly which one you need and why.
Turnaround times
State apostille (Virginia Secretary of State): 3–5 business days Federal apostille (U.S. Dept. of State): 5–10 business days Rush processing: sometimes available — ask when you reach out

The People's Rate
Community discount program. Two tiers. Honor system. Full rules documented below.
The governing rule: The discount follows the signer — not the person booking. Direct bookings only. Professional/platform bookings (title companies, attorneys, any third-party arrangement) always use standard rates, regardless of who the signer is.

Full eligibility rules
  • The discount follows the signer. The qualifying person must be the one signing the document — not the person booking on their behalf.
  • Direct bookings only. If the signing is arranged through a title company, attorney, or any professional platform, standard rates apply regardless of who the signer is.
  • Within 20 miles of downtown Richmond for the travel discount. Outside that radius, full travel fee applies. Tier 2 notary fee discount applies regardless of distance.
  • Honor system — no documentation required. Mention it when booking. The occasional bad actor isn't worth the friction of verification for everyone else.
  • One qualifying category is enough. No stacked discounts — someone who is both a veteran and an artist still gets Tier 1, not double.
  • Mention it at booking — not after. Discount cannot be applied retroactively once pricing is confirmed.
