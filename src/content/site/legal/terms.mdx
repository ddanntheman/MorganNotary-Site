These terms govern your use of this website and the services provided by Morgan Danner, Notary LLC ("we," "our," "us").

## Services

We provide mobile notary, loan signing, remote online notarization, apostille, and authentication services as described on this site. We are a commissioned Virginia notary public and, separately, a Certified Notary Signing Agent. We are **not** an attorney, and we do **not** provide legal advice, explain the legal effect of documents, interpret terms, or draft documents. For legal questions you must contact a licensed attorney.

## Pricing and payment

Prices are listed on our Pricing page. Virginia law caps the notarial act itself at $10 per signature (Va. Code § 47.1-19). Our package prices reflect travel, printing, scheduling, and witness coordination in addition to the statutory notarial fee. Payment is due at the time of service unless other arrangements are made in writing. For signings assigned through title companies and professional platforms, payment terms are established by the assigning company.

## Booking and cancellation

Appointments are confirmed by reply text or email. Cancellations made more than four hours before the scheduled appointment incur no fee. Cancellations within four hours may incur a trip fee equal to the published travel fee. If we cannot complete the signing because the signer cannot be properly identified, appears to lack capacity, refuses to sign willingly, or a required party is not present, the trip fee still applies.

## Unauthorized practice of law

In accordance with Virginia Code § 54.1-3900, a notary who is not a licensed attorney may not give legal advice, explain documents, or accept a fee for such advice. If during a signing you need legal information, we will recommend you contact your attorney or the document's preparer before proceeding.

Services are provided to the standard of a reasonable Virginia-commissioned notary. Our total liability for any claim related to a notarization is limited to the fee paid for that service, except where prohibited by law. We carry Errors & Omissions insurance as a backstop.
