**Last updated:** April 16, 2026

Morgan Danner, Notary LLC is committed to making this website accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities.

## Standard

This site is designed to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 at Level AA. We use semantic HTML, maintain 4.5:1 minimum contrast ratios, support keyboard-only navigation, and respect user preferences for reduced motion.

## Ongoing work

Accessibility is never "done." We run automated checks on every change, and we welcome feedback when something falls short. If you encounter a barrier, please email morgan@morgannotary.com with the page URL and a description. We will respond within five business days.

## Accessible service delivery

Beyond the website, we provide accessible service: we travel to you, we work at the signer's pace, we bring witnesses, and we accommodate communication preferences. If you need an accommodation for the signing itself — large-print copies, extended time, a specific location — mention it when you book.
