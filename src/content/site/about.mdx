## How I got here

There came a point where I looked at my career and thought: I want something that's mine. Something I built. Something where the quality of the work, and the experience for the client, is entirely up to me.

So I pivoted. I got my Virginia notary commission, my NNA certification, my E&O insurance. I trained as a loan signing agent and qualified for Remote Online Notarization. I did all of it intentionally, because if I was going to do this, I was going to do it right.

Morgan Danner, Notary isn't a side hustle. It's a business I built from scratch, in a city I love, for people who deserve better than a rushed signature and a "good luck with that."

> I wanted something that was mine, and I wanted it to actually mean something to the people I served.

## The mom part

I'm also a mom. And that matters, not because I want you to feel sorry for me, but because it's exactly why I work the way I do.

I know what it's like to have a packed schedule, a complicated life, and zero bandwidth for things that should be simple. I know what it's like to need something handled, correctly, quickly, without drama, so you can get back to the people who need you. That's what I'm here for.

The beaded necklace my kid made me is usually right there next to the notary seal. That's just who I am.

## Here's how I work.

1. **Details are not optional.** I check everything. Then I check it again. A missed signature or incorrect acknowledgment can hold up a closing or invalidate a document. That doesn't happen on my watch.

2. **I come to you.** Your hospital room, your office, your kitchen table. I bring everything, including a witness if you need one. You have enough to deal with.

3. **Mutual respect, always.** I don't care if you're signing a trust at 80 or a lease at 22. You get the same care, the same patience, and the same thorough walk-through of every page. Every time.

4. **Richmond is not just my market.** It's my community. That's why I offer [The People's Rate](/peoples-rate), discounted travel and reduced notary fees for veterans, LGBTQ+ folks, single parents, seniors, artists, activists, and immigrants who book directly with me. The discount follows you, the signer. Obviously I'm doing this. Why wouldn't I?

5. **I pick up the phone.** Call me, text me. Real response times. No autoresponder telling you to fill out a form. Just me, actually available.
