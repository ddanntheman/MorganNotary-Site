## The governing rule

The discount **follows the signer**, not the person booking. Direct bookings only. Professional/platform bookings (title companies, attorneys, any third-party arrangement) always use standard rates, regardless of who the signer is.

## Eligibility rules

- **Direct bookings only.** If the signing is arranged through a title company, attorney, or any professional platform, standard rates apply regardless of who the signer is.
- **Within 20 miles of downtown Richmond** for the travel-fee discount. Outside that radius, full travel fee applies. The Tier 2 notary-fee discount applies regardless of distance.
- **Honor system, no documentation required.** Mention it when booking. The occasional bad actor isn't worth the friction of verification for everyone else.
- **One qualifying category is enough.** No stacked discounts.
- **Mention it at booking**, not after. The discount cannot be applied retroactively once pricing is confirmed.
