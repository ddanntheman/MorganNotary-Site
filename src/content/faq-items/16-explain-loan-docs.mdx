question: Can you explain my loan documents?
answer: >-
  No — Virginia law prohibits notaries who are not licensed attorneys
  from explaining legal documents or giving legal advice. But I will
  direct you through every page, make sure you know where to sign and
  initial, and make sure you have your lender's number handy for
  anything about the terms themselves. If something is unclear, I'll
  pause and encourage you to call your lender or attorney before
  signing.
