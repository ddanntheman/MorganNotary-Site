question: How much does a mobile notary cost in Richmond?
answer: >-
  The notarial act itself is capped at $10 per signature by Virginia law
  (Va. Code § 47.1-19). My mobile service fee starts at $35 for the first
  act (travel + first signature), with additional acts billed at the $10
  statutory rate during the same visit. Loan signings, estate packages,
  RON, and apostille have their own flat-rate packages — see the pricing
  page for the full itemized sheet.
