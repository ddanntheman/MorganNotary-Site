question: Do I need a witness?
answer: >-
  It depends on the document. Wills, some trusts, certain estate documents,
  and some loan documents require one or two independent witnesses in
  addition to the notary. I can arrange a witness for you for an
  additional fee (included in the estate/trust and standard loan-signing
  packages). If you're not sure whether your document needs one, send me
  a photo of the signature block and I'll tell you.
