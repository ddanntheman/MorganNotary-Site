question: How does RON work step by step?
answer: >-
  You book a session, upload the document, verify your identity through
  credential analysis and a short knowledge-based authentication quiz,
  and then meet me on video. I walk through where to sign, you sign
  electronically, and I apply my digital notarial seal. The signed and
  notarized document is delivered to you immediately. The whole thing
  usually takes 15–20 minutes.
