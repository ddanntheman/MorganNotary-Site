question: What ID do I need?
answer: >-
  Virginia notaries are required to verify your identity with a current,
  government-issued photo ID. Acceptable forms include a Virginia
  driver's license, a U.S. passport or passport card, a military ID, or
  a state ID card. The name on the ID must match the name on the
  document. If your ID is expired or your name has changed, text me
  before the appointment and we'll figure it out.
