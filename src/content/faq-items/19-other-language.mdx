question: What if my document is in another language?
answer: >-
  I can notarize a document in any language as long as the signer
  understands it, Virginia law requires the signer (not the notary)
  to understand what's being signed. The notarial certificate itself
  must be in English. If your document needs a certified translation
  as well, I can connect you with a qualified translator.
