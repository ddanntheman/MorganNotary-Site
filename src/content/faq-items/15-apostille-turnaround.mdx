question: How long does an apostille take?
answer: >-
  Virginia state apostilles take 3–5 business days once the document
  is received by the Secretary of State. Federal apostilles from the
  U.S. Department of State take 5–10 business days. Rush processing
  is sometimes available, ask when you reach out.
