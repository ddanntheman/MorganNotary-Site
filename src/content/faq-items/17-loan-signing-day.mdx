question: What happens on loan-signing day?
answer: >-
  I arrive with the documents printed and organized. I verify your
  identification, direct you through each page showing exactly where
  to sign, initial, or date, and make sure nothing is skipped or
  signed incorrectly. I apply the notarial acts that are required,
  collect the signed package, and coordinate return delivery with the
  title company or lender. Most signings take 45–75 minutes.
