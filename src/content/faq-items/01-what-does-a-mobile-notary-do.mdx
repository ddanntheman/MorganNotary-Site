question: What exactly does a mobile notary do?
answer: >-
  A mobile notary is a commissioned notary public who travels to you, your
  home, office, hospital, or senior-living community, to witness signatures
  and administer oaths on legal documents. I don't explain legal terms or
  give advice; I verify your identity, confirm you are signing willingly,
  and apply my seal so the document holds up legally.
