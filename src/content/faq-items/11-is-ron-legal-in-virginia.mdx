question: Is online notary legal in Virginia?
answer: >-
  Yes. Virginia was one of the first states to authorize remote online
  notarization (Va. Code § 47.1-6.1). RON sessions performed by a
  Virginia-commissioned notary on an approved platform are legally
  recognized in Virginia and in every state that honors Virginia
  notarizations. You'll sign and I'll apply the digital seal, no
  paper required.
