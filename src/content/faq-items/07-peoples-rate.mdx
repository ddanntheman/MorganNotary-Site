question: What's The People's Rate and how do I qualify?
answer: >-
  The People's Rate is a community discount available on direct bookings
  for veterans, LGBTQ+ community members, single parents, artists,
  activists, seniors 65+, and immigrants / DACA recipients. Tier 1 is
  50% off the travel fee; Tier 2 is 50% off travel + 10% off the notary
  service fee. Honor system — no documentation required. Mention it
  when you book.
